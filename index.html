Frequently Asked Questions

How long does mehndi typically last?

Mehndi typically lasts 1-3 weeks depending on the quality of paste, your skin type, and aftercare. The color is darkest in the first 2-3 days and gradually fades afterward.

How can I make my mehndi darker?

For darker mehndi, leave the paste on for at least 6-8 hours. After removing, avoid water contact for 24 hours. Apply a mixture of lemon juice and sugar to dried mehndi. You can also warm your hands slightly to intensify the color.
